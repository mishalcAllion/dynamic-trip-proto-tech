Suggestion States

Explore the lifecycle of each suggested option

Recommended

This is the state you'll see when a suggested option is ready for you to review.

Selected

Once you approve an option with a thumbs up, the concierge will start the booking process for you.

Rejected

This is what happens when you thumbs down and reject an option.

Booked

Once the concierge confirms your booking, your reservation is finalized and all set!

Use the thumbs up/down buttons to change the status of each recommendation.

Reviewing Your Suggestions

Thumbs Up

By giving a thumbs up, you mark this option as Selected/Approved. Your concierge can then start booking this option.

Recommended → Selected

Thumbs Down

If you give a thumbs down, this option will be marked as Rejected. Your concierge will know not to move forward with this option.

Recommended → Rejected

This action will notify your concierge about your decision.
